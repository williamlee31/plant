PRODUCT

The Bloom device is a CC3200 Micro-controller made by Texas Instruments. These next few lines will list the specs of the microcontroller. Blah blah blah blah this controller is sick. It is capable of being hooked up to twenty differents sensors at a time.

LIGHT

Not sure how much time your plant spends in the light versus the shade while you're at work? Your bloom device records the levels of light exposed to your plant.

MOISTURE

Overwater your plants and you'll kill them. Underwater your plants and they die too. With Bloom's state of the art sensors, you always know the soil's moisture levels.

TEMPERATURE

Corroborating the temperature with other data on your plants, allows you to draw conclusions on how to best care for your plants. Was it the heat that caused your plant's soil's moisture levels to drop? Or does you plant need to drink more? Now you'll know.

FEATURES

USERPROFILE

Get your personal dashboard where you can add multiple Bloom devices, get weather alerts and monitor data regarding your plant's health.

DATA CHARTS

Simple, intuitive charts for your personal computer and mobile device. Get data on the moisture levels, tempetaure and amount of sunlight on your plants.

SETUP

  • POWER DEVICE

    After you recieve your device, power the device with batteries or powerup with the USB power cable.

  • SETUP SENSORS

    Insert the moisture sensor into the soil near your plant. Make sure that the light sensor at the top of your bloom device is not blocked by your plant.

  • REGISTER

    Create an account on this site. Then find your device ID that is packaged with your Bloom device. Enter your device ID on your User Profile page.

  • CONNECT

    Connect your device to Wi-Fi by entering your Wi-Fi network's information on the keypad on the side of your Bloom device.

  • MONITOR

    Watch the changes in values for moisture, light and temperature in your charts which can be found in your User Profile. Get updates for weather events and get custom text alerts.
